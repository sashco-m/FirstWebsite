2020 Behind the Scenes!

The best way to approach each year's challenge is through planning, experimenting, and most important of all, collaboration. In one month, we were able go from the drawing board to bringing our ideas to life, thanks to the dedication of team 4252. During this challenge, I learned several hands-on skills with tools and machinery, which I was able to pass on to younger students. Every step of the process, including design, building, redesign, and programming was an absolute joy, and I hope that the skills that I have taught to younger members of the club will allow them to carry on our legacy.
