The joys of woodworking

What started off as a cute Valentine's day project ignited my interest in woodworking. I have always felt an immense sense of accomplishment working with my hands, such as when playing guitar. I began with deciding to use pine, as it is a very soft and light wood, perfect ergonomically for earrings. I cut off thin slices on a bandsaw, and drew out a heart. After some time sanding, voila! I will be sure to continue to challenge myself in future projects
